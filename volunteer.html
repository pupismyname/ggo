Volunteer

We Depend on You

The dates for the 2013 Georgia Golden Olympics are fast approaching! For many years the volunteer GGO Planning Committee has be working with organizations throughout the state to promote an interest in lifetime sports, recreation and physical activity. From the most serious athlete to the determined participant, we offer a wide array of events that provide physical activity, fun and socialization. However, the success of this endeavor on the state level is GREATLY dependent on the help of our wonderful volunteers. I hope you will join us, and be a part of the 200+ people who will give so generously of their time to see and encourage living longer and living better through action, motion, fun and social interaction. You will be inspired.

This is a qualifying year for the National Senior Games. For more information on the 2013 National games visit www.nsga.com.

Volunteer T-shirts and Check In

Volunteer Shirt Pick-up: Check in and pick up the volunteer shirt on the morning of event at Senior Activity Center, 155 Maple Street, Warner Robins, GA. Please complete the volunteer form and sign the waiver.

Volunteer T-shirts, Certificates of Appreciation, maps, details of your event’s specific responsibilities will be given out on the morning of your event. Any questions you may have can be answered at that time. If you need further information, please contact Antuanette Davis (478-335-8094).
