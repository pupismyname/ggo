2013 Georgia Golden Olympics

The 2013 Georgia Golden Olympics will take place September 18-21, 2013 in Warner Robins, GA. The Warner Robins Recreation Department, under the direction of Mr. James Dodson, graciously agreed to host the 2013 event for the 12th consecutive year.

Anyone 50 and over may compete in the Georgia Golden Olympics by completing a registration form and sending required documentation of age with registration form to:

Georgia Golden Olympics
PO Box 958
Winder, GA 30680

Returning competitors can register online.

Deadline for Early Registration
August 1, 2013
Final Deadline for Registration
August 15, 2013

2013 National Senior Games

The 2013 National Senior Games Competition is scheduled for scheduled for July 21-August 6, 2013 in Cleveland, Ohio. The Greater Cleveland Sports Commission will be hosting the games and planning an exciting experience for athletes from all over the country. More information will be available as the date nears.

The 2012 Georgia Golden Olympics event was the national qualifying event for the 2013 National Competition.

For more information on the National Senior Games Association, visit www.nsga.com.
