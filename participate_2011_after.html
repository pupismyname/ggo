2011 Georgia Golden Olympics

The 2011 Georgia Golden Olympics took place September 21-24, 2011 in Warner Robins, GA. The Warner Robins Recreation Department, under the direction of Mr. James Dodson, graciously hosted the 2011 event for the 10th consecutive year.

Anyone 50 and over may compete in the Georgia Golden Olympics by completing a registration form and sending required documentation of age with registration form to:

Georgia Golden Olympics

Winder, GA 30680

2011 National Senior Games

The 2011 National Senior Games Competition is scheduled for scheduled for June 16-30, 2011 in Houston, Texas. A majority of the sports will be held at the George R. Brown Convention Center in the heart of downtown Houston and in areas closely surrounding the Center. The Houston LOC is very excited about hosting the 2011 event and have some great things planned for your visit.

The 2011 Georgia Golden Olympics event is not a national qualifying event. The 2012 Georgia event will be the qualifying event for the 2013 National Competition scheduled for July 21-August 6, 2013 in Cleveland, Ohio.

For more information on the National Senior Games Association, visit www.nsga.com.
