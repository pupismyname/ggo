The 2011 Georgia Golden Olympics took place September 21-24, 2011 in Warner Robins, Georgia. The 2011 Georgia event was not a national qualifying event. The 2012 Georgia Event will be the qualifying event for the 2013 National Senior Games in Cleveland, Ohio. For more information on the National Senior Games go to www.nsga.com.
